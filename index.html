About Us

Home Recovery Therapy Services

We help people recover in the place they are most comfortable — at home. Physical Therapy of the City P.C., doing business as Home Recovery Therapy Services, coordinates in-home therapy visits that connect patients with licensed clinicians and keep their care organized, timely, and secure.

Our Home Care platform — a mobile application and web console — is the operational backbone our team uses every day to schedule visits, match patients with the right providers, document care, and manage the details that make home-based therapy work smoothly. Behind the app is a team of licensed providers, service coordinators, and managers working together so that each patient’s visit happens on time and their records stay accurate and protected.

What We Do

We bring therapy to the patient’s door. Rather than asking people to travel while they are recovering, our providers deliver care where patients live. Our platform handles the coordination behind each visit so clinicians can focus on care:

  • Matching and scheduling patients with eligible, credentialed providers based on location, availability, and clinical fit.
  • Visit coordination from check-in to check-out, including precautions, home exercise plans, and follow-up.
  • Accurate documentation of each encounter, kept secure and available to the right members of the care team.
  • Streamlined payments, including insurance-based and self-pay options, handled through trusted processors.

Our Commitment to Privacy and Security

Health information is among the most sensitive data there is, and we treat it that way. Home Recovery Therapy Services is a HIPAA Covered Entity. We protect patient information with encryption, role-based access, audit logging, and workforce training, and we limit access to the minimum necessary for each person’s role. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy, and the rules governing use of our platform in our Terms and Conditions.
